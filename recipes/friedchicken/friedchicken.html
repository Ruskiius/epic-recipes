Fried Chicken Recipe Pollos Hermanos Style

By, Gustavo (Gus) Fring

This is a dish that requires precision, attention to detail, much like the operation of a successful business. Here is a recipe that will elevate your fried chicken to new heights, much like my own business, Los Pollos Hermanos:

Here's the list of ingredients you must have:

  • 4lbs of chicken pieces (legs, thighs, breasts)
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp of garlic powder
  • 2 tsp of onion powder
  • 2 tsp of paprika (add more for extra zest)
  • 1 tsp of cayenne pepper
  • Add salt and pepper as much as you desire
  • 2 cups of buttermilk
  • Oil for frying

Now it's time to put it all together:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  2. In a separate bowl, pour the buttermilk and stir to combine. Add the chicken pieces to the buttermilk, making sure each piece is well coated. Allow the chicken to marinate in the buttermilk for at least 2 hours in the refrigerator.
  3. Heat oil in a deep fryer or a large pot to 350°F.
  4. Remove the chicken pieces from the buttermilk and coat them in the flour mixture, shaking off any excess.
  5. Carefully add the chicken pieces to the hot oil, and fry them for about 12-15 minutes or until golden brown and cooked through to an internal temperature of 165°F.
  6. Remove the chicken from the oil and place it on a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.
